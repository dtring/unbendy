Have you ever felt nervous or intimidated to join a yoga class? Feel like you’re not flexible enough? Can’t touch your toes?

Me too! That’s why I decided to start Unbendy Yoga.

I believe that however flexible you are you can still benefit from the practice of yoga asana. I also try to incorporate ‘off the mat’ yogic practices into my teaching too, to give you the tools you need to access a happier, healthier you.

I run classes mostly online along with other events throughout the year. My aim is to help you work hard within the constraints of your own body, learning to love and nurture yourself through movement and practice.

About me

I’m Daisy and consider myself a forever-student of yoga. I’ve been practising myself for around 5 years and, as of May 2022, am a qualified yoga teacher (200hr with YogaHaven in Brighton).

Yoga is important to me because it has helped me to get myself in shape both mentally and physically, enabling me to cope better with the challenges of modern life and I’d love to share that with you.

My passion for yoga and wellbeing means I keep prices very low at £5 per person but I can also do concessions if needed.

Here's how it works...

  • For group sessions, see the schedule below or email me for more information and a Google Meets link
  • Make sure you have Google Meets set up on your computer or phone before the session start time
  • Fill in my waiver and disclaimer
  • Wear something you can move in, otherwise just bring a yoga mat and your willing self
  • That’s it!

Group classes are £5 per person. If you're interested in a 1-2-1 or in-person session, please email me directly.
